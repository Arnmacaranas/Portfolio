About Me

Hello! I'm Aaron Macaranas, a photographer & cinematographer with a deep passion for capturing moments that tell compelling stories. Whether it’s through a motion picture or a still frame, I’m driven by the power of visuals to evoke emotion, inspire thought, and connect people.

As a cinematographer, I specialize in crafting dynamic visual narratives for feature films, short films, commercials, and music videos. From lighting and composition to camera movement, I bring a cinematic approach to every project, collaborating with directors and production teams to create a distinct visual language that enhances the story.

As a photographer, photography is more than just capturing moments—it’s about preserving emotions, connections, and the beauty of the world around us. I strive to bring scripts to life by capturing moments that resonate with emotion, authenticity, and beauty. I specialize in portrait, wedding, and street photography, ensuring every photo is unique and meaningful.

My dual expertise in cinematography and photography gives me a unique perspective on storytelling, blending the art of stillness with the energy of motion. Whether working on a large-scale production or a personal project, I’m committed to delivering visuals that resonate deeply and leave a lasting impact.

Let’s create something extraordinary together!
